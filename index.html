Aggregate Scoring

The teams play two matches in Round of 16, Quarterfinal, and Semi-Final (home and away). The Aggregate Score is the combined total of goals from both games.

📌 Example

Game 1 ends 2–1  ·  Game 2 ends 1–1
Aggregate = 3–2. Most total goals advances.
